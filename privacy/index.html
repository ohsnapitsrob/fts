Privacy

Privacy at Find That Scene

Find That Scene is a personal fan project. The site is built to be lightweight, useful, and respectful of your privacy.

What this site collects

The site does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide personal information to browse locations, titles, or scenes.

Some basic technical information may be processed by the services needed to deliver the site, such as your browser, device, IP address, and the page you request.

Essential site features

Core features such as navigation, browsing, search, saved preferences, and the map are treated as essential to the experience.

The site may use local storage or temporary session storage on your device to remember simple preferences and interface state, such as privacy choices, dismissed prompts, or install prompt timing.

MapTiler

The map view uses MapTiler to display interactive maps. When you use the map, your browser connects to MapTiler so map tiles and related map data can load.

This is required for the map to work. If you do not want your browser to contact MapTiler, avoid opening the map view.

YouTube trailers and media embeds

Some title pages may include trailers or embedded media. These are optional and are blocked by default until you allow media embeds in the privacy settings.

If media embeds are not allowed, YouTube iframes are not added to the page and the trailer section is hidden entirely.

If media embeds are allowed, trailers are loaded using YouTube’s privacy-enhanced no-cookie embed domain where possible.

Analytics

The project may use Plausible Analytics to understand simple site usage, such as which pages are visited, which devices are used, and where visitors broadly come from.

Plausible is intended to be lightweight and privacy-friendly. The project does not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, advertising pixels, or cross-site tracking.

Your choices

You can manage optional media embeds at any time using the settings button in the top navigation, or by opening the settings here.

When you change your privacy settings, the site clears optional local storage and cookies it can access, then refreshes the page so the new choice is applied immediately.

Third-party content and rights

Posters, screenshots, logos, trailers, and related media remain the property of their respective owners. They are used for identification, commentary, and educational context within this independent fan project.

Find That Scene is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any studio, publisher, streaming platform, map provider, or rights holder.

Contact

If you have a privacy question, or you are a rights holder and would like content reviewed, credited differently, or removed, please get in touch at the project contact email.
